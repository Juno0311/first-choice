Coolest Accomplishments

I was the first trans Eagle Scout in Troop 7062, and I achieved the rank of Eagle in March of 2022. My project was to make Interactive Books for the SPED program. I attached QR codes which linked to videos and pictures. These videos and pictures showed verbs in action to help the students understand the books. These books are used in the classroom to promote critical thinking skills and language development

In my senior year of high school, I was chosen out of all Missouri high schools to speak at The National Governer's Association in Arkansas to talk about tech. I traveled to Bentonville, Arkansas where I was part of a student panel, discussing with state legislators from the Midwest, on how they can improve coding and IT classes in schools. We all came from different backgrounds so they could get the most information possible.
